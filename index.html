About me

Hi, I'm Lisa Lenkens, 30. Originally I worked in the social work industry with a degree in Social Care and Dutch Sign Language interpreting. I moved to London 2 years ago and I wanted to change my career. At my previous position I had a lot of spare time to think about what I really wanted in a new career and which direction I wanted to go.

That's when I decided to pursue Web Design. For me this is a perfect combination of different interests of mine. Creativity in my work is a must. Designing something from scratch, figuring out different styles, solving problems and eventually creating a user-friendly product or service is what I'm passionate about.

Check out my portfolio to see what kind of projects I've worked on so far and feel free to contact me.
